Currency & Money

  • 💰
    US Dollar (Primary Currency) The US dollar is the primary currency on Taniti. Most shops, restaurants, and services accept US dollars. Major credit cards are widely accepted at hotels and larger restaurants.
  • 💱
    Euros & Yen Also Accepted Many businesses also accept euros and Japanese yen. Currency exchange stations are available at the airport and in Merriton Landing. ATMs accepting international bank cards are located at major banks in Merriton Landing.

Language

  • 🗣
    English Widely Spoken Many younger Tanitians are fluent in English. In tourist areas such as Merriton Landing and Taniti City, English is commonly spoken in hotels, restaurants, and shops.
  • 📚
    Rural Areas In more rural parts of the island, English may be less common. Consider learning a few basic phrases or using a translation app if venturing off the beaten path.

Health & Medical

  • 🏥
    Hospital Taniti has one hospital equipped to handle emergencies and general medical care. Staff members speak multiple languages. A pharmacy is available on-site.
  • 🩺
    Medical Clinics Several clinics throughout the island provide routine medical care, vaccinations, and minor injury treatment. Multilingual staff available.
  • 💊
    Travel Insurance Travel insurance with medical coverage is strongly recommended for all visitors, especially those with pre-existing conditions. The hospital can coordinate with international insurance providers.

Safety

  • 🔒
    Generally Safe Violent crime is extremely rare on Taniti. However, as tourism has grown, petty crimes such as pickpocketing have increased. Take standard precautions with valuables.
  • 🍺
    Alcohol Regulations Alcohol cannot be served or sold between midnight and 9:00 AM. The legal drinking age is 18, though enforcement may vary.

Utilities & Power

  • ⚡
    120 Volts (US Standard) Power outlets in Taniti use 120-volt, 60 Hz electricity — the same as the United States. Visitors from the US will not need a power adapter.
  • 🔌
    International Visitors If you are traveling from Europe, Asia, or other regions, you may need a voltage converter and/or plug adapter. Check your device specifications before traveling.

National Holidays

  • 🎈
    Plan Around Holidays Taniti celebrates several national holidays throughout the year. Many attractions, shops, and restaurants close on these days. Check the holiday schedule when planning your visit to avoid disruptions.

Travel Documents

  • 📄
    Passport Required All international visitors must carry a valid passport. Check with your country's travel advisory for any visa requirements specific to Taniti before booking your trip.
