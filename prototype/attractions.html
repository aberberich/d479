Things to Do

From pristine beaches to rainforest adventures, Taniti has something for everyone.

Beaches & Water Activities

  • 🌊
    Sandy Beaches Family-Friendly Relax on Taniti's beautiful white sandy beaches along Yellow Leaf Bay. Perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and building sandcastles with the family.
  • 🤿
    Snorkeling Family-Friendly Explore vibrant coral reefs and tropical marine life in the crystal-clear waters surrounding the island. Equipment rentals available.
  • 🎣
    Chartered Fishing Tours Adventure Book a chartered deep-sea fishing excursion and experience the rich waters of the Pacific. Half-day and full-day options available.

Adventure & Nature

  • 🏔
    Rainforest Hiking Family-Friendly Trek through lush tropical rainforests with guided or self-guided trails. Experience exotic flora and fauna unique to the island.
  • 🌋
    Active Volcano Adventure Visit Taniti's active volcano in the mountainous interior. Guided tours take you to safe viewing areas with breathtaking panoramic views.
  • 🚀
    Zip-Lining Adventure Soar through the rainforest canopy on thrilling zip-line courses. Multiple difficulty levels available for beginners and experts alike.
  • 🚁
    Helicopter Tours Couples See the entire island from above with a scenic helicopter ride. Fly over the volcano, coastline, and rainforest canopy.

Culture & Leisure

  • 🏛
    Taniti Museum Family-Friendly Discover the rich history and culture of Taniti's indigenous people. Exhibits feature traditional art, tools, and the island's transformation over centuries.
  • 🎨
    Art Galleries Couples Browse local and visiting art collections in several galleries around Merriton Landing. Featuring paintings, sculptures, and traditional crafts.
  • 🎳
    Bowling & Arcade Family-Friendly Enjoy indoor entertainment at the island's bowling alley and arcade — great for families and rainy-day activities.
  • 🏋
    Boat & Bus Tours Family-Friendly Take guided boat tours around the island coast or hop on a bus tour of Taniti City to see its native architecture and landmarks.

Nightlife & Entertainment

  • 🍺
    Pubs & Microbrewery Budget-Friendly Sample locally-brewed craft beers at Yellow Leaf Brewing Co. or relax at one of several pubs in Merriton Landing. Note: Alcohol is not sold between midnight and 9:00 AM.
  • 💃
    Dance Club Dance the night away at Club Coral, Taniti's popular dance club on the waterfront. Live DJs on weekends and a vibrant atmosphere make for a memorable evening. Open Thursday through Saturday, 9:00 PM to midnight.
  • 🎬
    Movie Theater Family-Friendly Catch the latest films at the Taniti Cinema in Merriton Landing — a great way to unwind after a day of exploring.
