Accommodation Options

🏘

Taniti Bay Resort & Spa Couples

Taniti's premier four-star resort offers luxury amenities, ocean views, on-site dining, spa services, and organized activities for guests of all ages.

🏠

Family-Owned Hotels Family-Friendly

Several family-run hotels such as the Merriton Inn and Harbor View Hotel provide comfortable rooms with local charm, centrally located near restaurants and activities.

🌱

Bed & Breakfasts Couples

A growing number of B&Bs including Yellow Leaf Cottage and Hillside Haven offer a personal, home-away-from-home experience with breakfast included.

🛌

Taniti Backpackers Hostel Budget-Friendly

Budget-friendly hostel with shared dorms and private rooms. Social common area, lockers, and a great base for solo travelers and backpackers.

Booking Tips

  • 📅
    Book Early Taniti's tourism is growing rapidly. Popular accommodations, especially the resort and B&Bs, fill up quickly during peak season.
  • 📍
    Location Matters Staying near Merriton Landing puts you close to restaurants, shops, and public transportation. Beach-side options are great for a quieter experience.
  • 👪
    Traveling with Family? Family hotels and B&Bs often accommodate children and can arrange local activity recommendations.
  • 💰
    On a Budget? The Taniti Backpackers Hostel offers the most affordable rates on the island. Dorms and private rooms available — book online or walk in.
