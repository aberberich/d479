Dining

Savor fresh local flavors and international cuisine across Taniti's 10 restaurants.

Local Tanitian Cuisine

Five restaurants specialize in traditional Tanitian dishes featuring freshly caught fish, locally grown rice, and tropical fruits. These family-run establishments offer an authentic taste of island culture.

  • 🍣
    The Yellow Leaf Kitchen Located on the waterfront in Merriton Landing. Known for its signature grilled reef fish with coconut rice. $$
  • 🍣
    Mama Tani's A beloved family restaurant in Taniti City serving traditional fish and rice dishes with homemade tropical sauces. $
  • 🌴
    Bay Harvest Farm-and-ocean-to-table dining near Yellow Leaf Bay. Seasonal menus featuring local root vegetables and fresh-caught seafood. $$
  • 🌴
    Coral Reef Grill Casual beachside eatery with grilled fish platters, tropical fruit bowls, and fresh-squeezed juices. $
  • 🍴
    Volcano View Bistro Upscale Tanitian dining with panoramic views of the island interior. Reservations recommended for dinner. $$$

American-Style Dining

Three restaurants offer familiar American-style menus including burgers, steaks, salads, and classic comfort food — perfect for families and visitors seeking a taste of home.

  • 🍔
    The Landing Grill Family-Friendly Burgers, fries, and milkshakes in the heart of Merriton Landing. Kid-friendly menu available. $$
  • 🍖
    Shoreline Steakhouse Steaks, seafood, and salads with a waterfront view. A popular spot for dinner. $$$
  • 🍔
    Palmside Diner Budget-Friendly Casual all-day breakfast and lunch spot near the harbor. Great value for travelers on a budget. $

Pan-Asian Cuisine

Two Pan-Asian restaurants offer a diverse menu of dishes from across Asia, blending flavors from Chinese, Japanese, Thai, and Vietnamese traditions with fresh island ingredients.

  • 🍚
    Pacific Rim Kitchen Sushi, ramen, and stir-fries prepared with locally sourced seafood. Reservations recommended for dinner service. $$
  • 🥣
    Jade Garden Thai curries, Vietnamese pho, and Chinese favorites in a relaxed setting near Merriton Landing. $$

Groceries & Convenience

If you prefer to cook your own meals or need to pick up snacks and essentials, Taniti has several options:

  • 🛒
    Two Supermarkets Full-service supermarkets with fresh produce, meats, seafood, and imported goods.
  • 🌮
    Two Smaller Grocery Stores Neighborhood stores carrying everyday essentials and local specialties.
  • 🌙
    24-Hour Convenience Store Open around the clock for late-night snacks, drinks, toiletries, and basic necessities.
